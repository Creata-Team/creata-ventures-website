DeepTech / Industrial / Materials Science

🔩ALUMINICO

Revolutionary Hot Aluminizing Technology for Structural Steel. First horizontal aluminizing process using MHD locks — 64% cheaper than zinc galvanizing.

The Problem

Corrosion costs the global economy $2.2 trillion annually. Aluminum coating outperforms zinc, but hot aluminizing only works for flat sheets — not shaped structural products like pipes and beams.

Our Solution

ALUMINICO invented MHD (magnetohydrodynamic) locks enabling horizontal aluminizing:

  • 64% cost reduction: $53/ton vs $147.5/ton for zinc galvanizing
  • 9x smaller bath size: 2.5 m³ vs 24.3 m³
  • 62% less metal consumption: 23.3 kg/ton vs 61.7 kg/ton
  • Superior corrosion protection with aluminum coating

BioTech / HealthTech / Genomics

🧬AGC Genomics

Kazakhstan's first local next-generation sequencing (NGS) genetic testing platform. Bringing affordable, accessible genomic diagnostics to a 20M+ population with less than 1% market penetration.

The Problem

Kazakhstan lacks local NGS genetic testing infrastructure. All samples are sent abroad — increasing costs ($600–$2,500 per test), turnaround time (4–6 weeks), and creating data sovereignty risks. Less than 1% of the population has access to genetic diagnostics.

Our Solution

AGC Genomics establishes a full-cycle local NGS laboratory with international technology partnerships:

  • 4 product lines: Prenatal screening (NIPT), whole exome sequencing (WES), colorectal cancer screening (COLOTECT), preimplantation genetic testing (PGT-A)
  • 30–50% cost reduction vs. sending samples abroad
  • Turnaround time: 5–10 business days (vs. 4–6 weeks)
  • Local data processing compliant with Kazakhstan regulations
